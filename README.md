# ARCADE//01

ARCADE//01 is a collection of old-school arcade games made with the Godot Engine.

## Games

### Asteroid Void

Try to survive in a never-ending asteroid storm. Move your ship across lanes and avoid hitting asteroids. The longer you survive the faster the game gets.

### Tank Duel

Fight against a tank controlled by the computer. Use cover to protect yourself avoid getting hit and destroy the enemy tank before you run out of health.

## Features

* School black and white graphics

* Many arcade games, in one project

* Computer-controlled enemy

* Track your time

* Play with keyboard

* Play on itch.io

## Controls

### Menu

* Up Arrow. Move up

* Down Arrow. Move down

* Enter. Choose

### Asteroid Void

* Left Arrow. Move left

* Right Arrow. Move right

### Tank Duel

* A. Move left

* D. Move

* Space. Shoot

## Built With

* Godot Engine 4.6

* GDScript

## Play Online

You can play it on itch.io.
